Exercise 16-028 Algo (Assessing the Model)

Attempting to analyze the relationship between advertising and sales, the owner of a furniture store recorded the monthly advertising budget ($thousands) and the sales ($millions) for a sample of 12 months.

Advertising 23 46 60 54 28 33 25 31 36 88 90 99
Sales 9.6 11.4 12.6 9.7 8.7 12.4 11.9 11.4 12.7 13.5 14.5 15.7

(a) Determine the standard error of estimate.

1.373 thousand

(b) Is there evidence of a linear relationship between advertising and sales? (Use a 5% significance level.)

H0: β1 = 0

H1: β1 ≠ 0

Test statistic: 3.79

p-value: 0.0036

There is enough evidence to infer a linear relationship.

(c) Determine a confidence interval estimate of β1. (Use a 95% confidence level.)

LCL = 0.0236

UCL = 0.0908

(d) Compute the coefficient of determination.

R2 = 0.5892

58.92% of the variation in sales is explained by the variation in advertising.

(e) Briefly summarize what you have learned in parts (a) through (d).

ii

There is significant evidence that a linear relationship between advertising and sales exists, and the confidence interval suggests that the relationship is positive.
