Exercise 07-034 Algo (Random Variables and Probability Distributions)

You have been given the choice of receiving $200 in cash or receiving a gold coin that has a face value of $100. However, the actual value of the gold coin depends on its gold content. You are told that the coin has a 30% probability of being worth $300, a 30% probability of being worth $700, and a 40% probability of being worth its face value. Using your decision on expected value, should you choose the coin?

The expected value of the coin is $340. Since the expected value of the coin is greater than $200, the coin should be chosen.

Exercise 07-124 Algo (Binomial Distribution)

According to a poll, 51% of American adults think that protecting the environment should be given priority over developing U.S. energy supplies, 33% percent think that developing energy supplies is more important, and 6% believe the two are equally important. The rest had no opinion. Suppose that a sample of 100 American adults is quizzed on the subject.

(a) What is the probability fifty or more think that protecting the environment should be given priority?

0.6181

(b) What is the probability thirty or fewer think that developing energy supplies is more important?

0.3006

(c) What is the probability three or fewer have no opinion?

0.0078
