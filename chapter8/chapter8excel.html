Excel Activity: Calculating Probability of the Completion Time of the Project

In preparing to launch a new product, a marketing manager has determined the critical path for the department. The activities and the mean and variance of the completion time for each activity along the critical path are shown in the accompanying table.

Activity Expected Completion Time (Days) Variance
Develop survey questionnaire 10 2
Pretest the questionnaire 11 7
Revise the questionnaire 5 2
Hire survey company 2 1
Conduct survey analyze data 30 9
Analyze data 32 11
Prepare report 13 4

The data has been collected in the Microsoft Excel file below. Download the spreadsheet and perform the required analysis to answer the questions below. Do not round intermediate calculations. Round your answers to four decimal places.

Spreadsheet file: Download spreadsheet Pr08-93-c94728.xlsx

(a) Find the probability that the launch of the new product takes more than 109 days.
