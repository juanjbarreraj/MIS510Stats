Exercise 09-031 Algo (Sampling Distribution of the Mean)

The amount of time spent by North American adults watching television per day is normally distributed with a mean of 5 hours and a standard deviation of 1.4 hours.

(a) What is the probability that a randomly selected North American adult watches television for more than 6 hours per day?

0.2375

(b) What is the probability that the average time watching television by a random sample of five North American adults is more than 6 hours?

0.0551

(c) What is the probability that in a random sample of five North American adults, all watch television for more than 6 hours per day?

0.0008

(a) What is the probability distribution of the mean of the sample of four homes?

The sample mean is normally distributed with a mean of 2600 and a standard deviation of 100.

Exercise 09-043 Algo (Sampling Distribution of the Mean)

Exercise 09-060 Algo (Sampling Distribution of a Proportion)

What does this tell you about the professor's claim?

The claim appears to be false.
